Our Services

Official barangay documents processed quickly and transparently for residents of Barangay Sirangan.

Barangay Clearance

Official clearance certifying you are a resident in good standing with no derogatory records in the barangay. Required for employment, legal transactions, and government applications.

1–2 Business Days

Certificate of Residency

Certifies that you are a legitimate resident of Barangay Sirangan, Sorsogon City. Required for school enrollment, scholarship applications, and other official purposes.

Same Day

Certificate of Indigency

Certifies that you belong to an indigent or low-income family. Required for medical assistance, scholarship grants, and other social welfare programs.

Same Day

Business Permit Endorsement

Barangay endorsement required for business permit applications at the city hall. Applicable to new and renewing business owners operating within Barangay Sirangan.

2–3 Business Days

Official documentation of incidents, disputes, accidents, or complaints filed with the barangay. Used for legal purposes and community safety records.
