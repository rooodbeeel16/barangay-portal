History

Barangay Sirangan is one of the barangays comprising Sorsogon City in the province of Sorsogon, located in the Bicol Region of the Philippines. The barangay has a rich history rooted in the agricultural and coastal traditions of the Bicolano people.

The name "Sirangan" is derived from the local dialect, reflecting the cultural heritage of its founding residents. Over the generations, the community has grown from a small settlement into a thriving barangay with a dedicated local government committed to the welfare of its residents.

Today, Barangay Sirangan continues to advance through responsible governance, community programs, and digital innovations such as this portal — ensuring that residents receive fast, transparent, and accessible public services.
